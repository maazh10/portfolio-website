Another World Cup comes to a close, and what a historic one it was for our city and country. It was a month filled with excitement, anticipation, and joy. Watching people from all over the world come together to support their favorite teams and players is always inspiring. It shows the power of this beautiful game to unite people, regardless of their background or nationality.

Although I wasn't fortunate enough to witness a match in person, I was able to experience the energy and passion at the heart of the city. With Toronto's incredible cultural diversity, it was undoubtedly one of the best host cities of the tournament. Truly the world in a city.

Some photos here to look back at the month-long celebration of football in Toronto.
